Gallery

This is a Gallery component. It can behave as a lightbox (when given the lightbox class), and you can customise its appearance with a numbers of modifiers, as well as assing it an aptional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details)

Items

This is an Items component, and it's essentially a grid for organising elements of various types. You can customize it appearance with a choice of modifiers, as well as assing it an optional onload or onscroll transition modifier (details)
